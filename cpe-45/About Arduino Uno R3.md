Operating Voltage:
- Whole Board: 5V
- Microcontroller: 3.7V

Input Voltage: 
- recommended = 7-12V
- limit = 6 - 20V

Digital I/O Pins: 14 (6 PWM)
Analog Input Pins: 6

DC Current:
- per I/O Pin = 20mA
- for 3.3V = 50mA
